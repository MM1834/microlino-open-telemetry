# Code of Conduct

Microlino Open Telemetry aims to be a friendly, constructive and inclusive open-source project.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Sharing knowledge openly
- Explaining decisions clearly
- Welcoming newcomers
- Accepting that reverse engineering often involves uncertainty

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Disrespectful language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Intentionally misleading technical claims

## Technical Discussions

CAN decoding and vehicle telemetry can involve incomplete information.

Please distinguish clearly between:

- confirmed findings
- assumptions
- hypotheses
- experimental results

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation if behavior is harmful to the project or community.
